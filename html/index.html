Our Story

Turkish chef Mehmet Burak Ã–zdemir, born in 1994 in the culinary-rich city of Antakya, rose from his family's restaurant business to become a global internet sensation. Inheriting a passion for cooking from his father and grandfather, he began his career at a young age in Istanbul. It was his unique approach on social media that catapulted him to fame; by preparing enormous-sized Turkish and Arab dishes with a constant, wide smile aimed directly at the camera, he created a viral brand. His captivating videos on Instagram and TikTok attracted millions of followers, turning his restaurants into must-visit destinations for celebrities and food enthusiasts from around the world.

Capitalizing on his digital fame, Chef Burak expanded his "CZNBurak" restaurant chain from its Turkish roots to major international hubs like Dubai and Doha. However, his journey to building a global empire was met with a significant personal and professional crisis. He publicly accused his father of selling the rights to his brand name without his consent, a dispute that led to legal action. This challenging chapter revealed the adversities behind his public smile, forcing him to announce a new, independent start to his career.

Beyond his business success and challenges, Chef Burak is widely admired for his humanitarian spirit. He is well-known for his charitable activities, consistently sharing his large-scale meals with those in need and launching initiatives to support refugees and children in various communities. His story is not just one of a talented chef who achieved fame, but of an ambitious and resilient individual who used his platform to create a positive impact, inspiring millions with his creativity, generosity, and perseverance.
